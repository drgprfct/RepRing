## Description

RepRing is a simple iPhone app for building a daily crunch and push-up habit.

Track your reps from one compact Today screen, tune your default set sizes and daily goals with simple dials, and review your recent history at a glance. Optional reminders help you fit small sessions into the day. Optional Apple Health export saves your daily reps as a strength-training workout when you choose to connect Health.

RepRing is local-first: no account, no ads, no third-party analytics SDK, and no social feed. Just count your reps, day after day.

## Review Notes

HealthKit is optional. RepRing writes the user's daily crunch and push-up total as a `traditionalStrengthTraining` workout only after the user grants Health permission. RepRing stores exact rep counts locally on device. Auto-export updates one RepRing workout per day rather than creating duplicate workouts. RepRing does not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or third-party sharing.
