Your Data

RepRing is designed to work without an account. Current v1 app data is stored locally on your device, except for optional Apple Health export that you control through HealthKit permissions. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Apple Health

Apple Health export is optional. If enabled, RepRing writes your daily reps as a strength-training workout and may replace the same day's previous RepRing workout so your Health app does not accumulate duplicate RepRing workouts.

You should review exported workouts for accuracy. Do not rely on RepRing's exported HealthKit data as a clinical or physiological measurement.

RepRing reminders are local iOS notifications. They are intended as simple habit nudges and are not urgent alerts. You can disable reminders in RepRing or in iOS Settings.
