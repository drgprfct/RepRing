RepRing

Support

RepRing tracks daily crunches and push-ups locally on your iPhone, with optional reminders and optional Apple Health export.

Quick Checks

  • If reminders do not appear, check iOS Settings → Notifications → RepRing.
  • If Apple Health export does not work, check iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → RepRing.
  • If your counts look wrong, use the reset button on Today to clear today's reps and log them again.

Report A Problem

Open a support issue at github.com/drgprfct/RepRing/issues. Include your app version, iOS version, device model, and the steps that caused the issue.

Privacy

Do not post private health details in public support issues. Read the Privacy Policy for how RepRing handles local app data and optional Apple Health export.
