RepRing

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Summary

RepRing is designed as a local-first fitness habit app. It does not use accounts, advertising, third-party analytics SDKs, third-party crash SDKs, or a RepRing server.

Data Stored On Device

RepRing stores your daily crunch and push-up counts, set sizes, goals, reminder settings, appearance preference, Apple Health auto-export preference, and the latest local Apple Health export signature on your device.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, RepRing schedules local iOS notifications. Reminder times stay on your device and can be changed or disabled in the app or in iOS Settings.

Apple Health

Apple Health export is optional. If you grant HealthKit permission, RepRing can write your daily reps as a strength-training workout and can inspect previous RepRing workouts for the same day so it updates one RepRing workout instead of creating duplicates.

RepRing does not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or third-party sharing.

Data Collection

RepRing does not transmit your app data to a RepRing server and does not share your data with third-party partners.

Deleting Data

You can reset today's reps in the app. You can remove local RepRing app data by deleting the app from your device. Apple Health permissions and exported workouts are managed through Apple Health and iOS Settings.

Support

For help, use the RepRing support page. Do not include private health details in public support requests.
